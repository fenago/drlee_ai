
# DrLee.ai Brand Package - V3 Rigorous Edition

## Overview

This package contains the complete, rigorous brand foundation for DrLee.ai, designed to serve two distinct but related audiences: **The Career Advancer** and **The Founder/Builder**. The brand is inspired by a fusion of **MasterClass.com** (cinematic, aspirational storytelling) and **MIT/Stanford Executive Education** (intellectual rigor, academic prestige).

All documents in this package have been created with significantly more depth and rigor than typical brand documents, matching the standards of comprehensive strategic consulting deliverables.

## Package Contents

### 1. **Problem_Aware_Avatars_V3_Rigorous.md** (2,000+ words)
This document contains two deeply researched customer avatars:

- **Avatar 1: The Career Advancer ("Alex Chen")** - A talented developer stuck in the tutorial trap, desperate to land a high-paying AI role. Investment range: $500-$3,000. ROI: 100x (course → salary).
- **Avatar 2: The Founder/Builder ("Sarah Jones")** - A visionary founder who needs to build a defensible AI product without a massive engineering team. Investment range: $3,000-$15,000+. ROI: 1000x (course → company value).

Each avatar includes: Executive Summary, Demographics, Psychographics & Mindset, Pain Points & Frustrations, Goals & Desires, and a detailed "Day in the Life" narrative.
