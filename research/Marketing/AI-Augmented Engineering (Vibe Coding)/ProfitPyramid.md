### For Engineers:
- Free tier builds awareness and trust
- Self-paced validates the approach ($2K test)
- Cohort drives career transformation ($7K investment → $100K salary increase)
- Founder's Edition for those building practices/leading orgs

### For Founders:
- Free tier addresses skepticism with ROI focus
- Self-paced proves technical viability
- Cohort delivers team implementation and massive savings
- Founder's Edition for scaling and strategic advantage

### Ascension Strategy:
- **Content Marketing:** Drive Free → Self-Paced (10% for engineers, 5% for founders)
- **Success Stories:** Drive Self-Paced → Cohort (20% for engineers, 30% for founders)
- **Transformation Results:** Drive Cohort → Founder's Edition (15-20%)
- **Ongoing Relationship:** Founder's Edition clients become long-term partners

### Retention:
- Self-Paced: Lifetime access, recurring content updates
- Cohort: 4 times per year, building alumni network
- Founder's Edition: 6-12 month engagements with renewal potential
- All levels build community and network effects
