## PRODUCT LADDER SUMMARY

### Pricing Progression:
- **Tier 1 (Attract):** $0 - Build list, establish authority
- **Tier 2 (Nurture):** $119-$297 - Tripwire, qualify buyers
- **Tier 3 (Strategy):** $497-$997 - Discovery, personalized roadmap
- **Tier 4 (Core):** $1,997-$6,997 - Main transformation program
- **Tier 5 (High):** $19,997-$49,997 - Premium with implementation support
- **Tier 6 (5%):** $49,997-$1,000,000+ - Enterprise and strategic

### Expected Customer Journey:

**For Engineers (Individual Contributors):**
1. Download "SLM vs LLM Framework" (Free) → Email list
2. Buy "SLM Starter Kit" ($119) → Qualify as serious learner
3. (Optional) "Strategy Session" ($497) → Career roadmap
4. Enroll in "Cohort" ($6,997) → Complete transformation
5. (Optional) Upgrade to "Founder's Edition" ($19,997) → 1:1 support for job search
6. **Lifetime Value:** $119 → $7,113 → $19,997+ = **$20K-$27K LTV**

**For Founders (Bootstrapped → Funded):**
1. Use "API Cost Calculator" (Free) → Email list
2. Book "API Cost Audit" ($997) → Migration roadmap
3. Enroll technical founder in "Cohort" ($6,997) → Learn fundamentals
4. Hire "Fractional CTO" ($49,997-$99,997) → Implement first SLM
5. Post-raise: "Enterprise Team Training" for 20-50 engineers ($119,997) → Scale team
6. **Lifetime Value:** $997 → $7,994 → $107,991 → $500K+ (ongoing advisory) = **$100K-$600K+ LTV**

**Tier 1 → Tier 2:** "You've learned the WHY, now learn the HOW - get your first SLM running this weekend"

**Tier 2 → Tier 3:** "You have the basics, now get your custom roadmap - stop guessing, start executing"

**Conservative Model:**
- Tier 1: 2,000 leads/month × 12 = 24,000 leads (acquisition asset)

- Tier 3: 10% × 1,200 = 120 buyers × $750 avg = **$90,000**

### Key Success Factors:

1. **Lead Generation:** Free content must provide massive value and clear CTA
2. **Tripwire Conversion:** 5-10% of leads should buy Tier 2 within 30 days
3. **Core Product Conversion:** 30-40% of Tier 3 buyers should enroll in Tier 4
4. **Premium Ascension:** 10-20% of Tier 4 buyers should upgrade to Tier 5/6
5. **Lifetime Value:** Focus on ascending customers through ladder, not one-time sales

The Product Ladder is designed to:
- **Meet customers where they are** with appropriate pricing and format
- **Build trust incrementally** through low-risk entry points
- **Create natural ascension** where each tier solves problems revealed by previous tier
- **Maximize lifetime value** by serving customers across their entire journey
- **Generate referrals** at every tier (satisfied customers promote to peers)

The ultimate goal: Turn free content consumers into $100K-$500K+ lifetime customers by delivering transformation at every level.
