## MESSAGE TESTING FRAMEWORK

### Test Your Message Against These Criteria:

✅ **Specificity:** Does it use concrete metrics? (90% cost reduction, $300K salary, 64x efficiency)
✅ **Differentiation:** Does it explain what makes this unique? (ONLY, first, complete end-to-end system)
✅ **Transformation:** Does it show clear before/after? (API user → Architect, $500K/month → $50K/month)
✅ **Urgency:** Does it create FOMO or time pressure? (Competitors building moats, valuations diverging)
✅ **Credibility:** Does it include proof or authority? (Powers OpenAI/Anthropic, practitioners who shipped)
✅ **Emotional Resonance:** Does it hit a pain or aspiration? (Commoditization fear, ownership pride)
✅ **Clarity:** Can a 12-year-old understand it? (Build models, stop renting, own your tech)
