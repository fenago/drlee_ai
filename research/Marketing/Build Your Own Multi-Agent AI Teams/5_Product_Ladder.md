## PRODUCT LADDER MANAGEMENT

### Pricing Strategy
- **Value-Based Pricing:** Based on ROI (engineers: $100K+ salary increases; founders: $500K-$5M funding unlocked)
- **Tiered Options:** Engineer vs. Founder editions at each tier to maximize revenue per customer
- **Payment Plans:** Reduce friction for high-ticket items ($997+ products)
- **Annual Price Increases:** 10-15% annually to account for content updates and market positioning

### Discounts & Promotions
- **Early Bird:** 20% off cohorts/bootcamps for early enrollment (60+ days out)
- **Team Discounts:** 15% off for 2-3 people, 25% off for 4-5 people, 30% off for 6+ people
- **Alumni Upgrades:** 30% off higher-tier products for past customers
- **Scholarship Program:** 2-3 full scholarships per cohort for underrepresented groups

### Quality Control
- **Customer Success Metrics:** Track completion rates, project deployments, salary increases, funding outcomes
- **NPS Scoring:** Target 70+ NPS across all products
- **Continuous Improvement:** Quarterly content updates based on student feedback
- **Instructor Training:** Ensure consistent quality across multiple instructors (if scaling)
