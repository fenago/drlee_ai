## Key Insights

1. **The ladder is a journey, not a catalog** - Position each step as the natural next progression
2. **Bundle savings increase with commitment** - Reward those who commit to complete tracks
3. **Price anchoring works** - Show the "regular" price vs. bundle savings
4. **Scarcity and urgency matter** - Limited cohort spots, early-bird pricing, bonuses
5. **The top 5% drive 50% of revenue** - Optimize for ascension, not just acquisition
6. **Payment plans reduce friction** - 3-6 month payment plans increase conversions 30-40%
7. **Employer reimbursement unlocks budgets** - Provide invoices and training documentation
8. **The ladder never ends** - Alumni can return for new courses, refreshers, consulting
