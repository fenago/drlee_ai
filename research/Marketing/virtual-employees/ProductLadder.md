## Ascension Flow Strategy

### From Free → $997 (Self-Paced)
**Primary Path**:
1. Download Blueprint (FREE)
2. Weekly emails with case studies
3. Attend free webinar
4. Purchase Starter Kit ($197) OR
5. Book strategy call (FREE) → Self-Paced ($997)

**Conversion Goal**: 5% of blueprint downloads → paid customers

---

### From $997 → $3,997 (Cohort Upgrade)
**Primary Path**:
1. Complete 2-3 modules self-paced
2. Hit roadblock or want community
3. Offer cohort upgrade ($3,000 credit from $997)
4. Join next cohort with foundation already built

**Conversion Goal**: 20% of self-paced → cohort upgrade

---

### From $3,997 → $9,997 (Founder's Edition Upgrade)
**Primary Path**:
1. Mid-cohort (Week 4-5)
2. Identify students with complex needs
3. Offer 1-on-1 consulting upgrade
4. Position as "insurance" for critical deployment

**Conversion Goal**: 10% of cohort → Founder's Edition

---

### From $9,997 → $49,997+ (Enterprise Implementation)
**Primary Path**:
1. Founder's Edition students with success
2. Want to expand to entire organization
3. Pitch team license or full implementation
4. Build case study for marketing

**Conversion Goal**: 5% of Founder's Edition → Enterprise

---

### From Any Tier → $97-$997/month (Recurring)
**Primary Path**:
1. Course graduates at any level
2. 30-60 days post-completion
3. Email campaign highlighting alumni benefits
4. Offer monthly support for ongoing success

**Conversion Goal**: 30% of graduates → recurring membership
